# Development Philosophy

System Pulse follows one development cycle.

Observe.

Build.

Use.

Learn.

Refine.

Repeat.

The founder is the first customer.

Reality always overrides assumptions.

Do not optimise for imagined users.

Optimise for solving the founder's daily problem.

Version One should become something the founder genuinely relies on every day.

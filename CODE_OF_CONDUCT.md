# Code of Conduct

System Pulse should be a respectful, focused engineering environment.

## Expected Behavior

Contributors are expected to:

- communicate clearly and respectfully;
- assume good intent while still asking precise questions;
- keep feedback specific to the work;
- document decisions and uncertainty;
- avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or intimidation;
- discriminatory comments or conduct;
- threats or abusive language;
- publishing private information without permission;
- repeated disruption of project work after being asked to stop.

## Enforcement

The project owner may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that do not align with this code of conduct.

A fuller governance and moderation process may be adopted later.
